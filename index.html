FAQ

Can we bring children?

You are more than welcome to bring your children, as our little one will be in attendance! We also understand if you'd rather come alone, so you can have a night off! Whatever you decide, let us know when you RSVP so we know who to cater for.

Confetti

If you're bringing confetti then please make sure it's white, cream or ivory.

Transport

If travelling by car, there is plenty of parking at the venue. There will also be a parking attendant there to tell you where to go. If you are travelling by train, the nearest station is Farnham, which is 5 minutes from the castle by taxi. Here are some local taxi numbers if you wish to book any taxis in advance.

Farnham Taxi - 01252 856449
Farnham Station Taxis - 01252 735735
Home James Taxis - 01252 737475
Blueline Taxis - 01252 856244

Personal belongings

There are hooks for your coats near the ceremony room. If you are checking into a local hotel, or if you are staying at Farnham Castle and have suitcases, it's probably a good idea to leave them in the car until you are able to check in. If you are not arriving by car, let us know and the venue will store them in a safe place for you.

Is anything happening the morning after the wedding?

Please feel free to join us for a drink at a pub, we'll be there from about midday.
